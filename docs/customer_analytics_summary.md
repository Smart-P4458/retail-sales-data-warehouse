# Customer Analytics Summary

## Project

Retail Sales Data Warehouse

---

# Objective

The purpose of this analysis is to understand customer purchasing behaviour, identify high-value customers, evaluate customer retention, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

# Business Questions Answered

1. Who are our highest-value customers?
2. How frequently do customers purchase?
3. How many customers are repeat buyers?
4. How many customers purchase only once?
5. Which countries generate the highest customer revenue?
6. Can customers be segmented based on spending?

---

# Analyses Performed

## 1. Top Customers by Lifetime Revenue

### Description

Calculated the total revenue generated by each customer across all purchases.

### Business Value

Identifies VIP customers who contribute the most revenue.

### SQL Concepts Used

- INNER JOIN
- GROUP BY
- SUM()
- ORDER BY
- LIMIT

---

## 2. Customer Order Frequency

### Description

Calculated the number of unique orders placed by each customer.

### Business Value

Measures customer loyalty and purchasing frequency.

### SQL Concepts Used

- COUNT(DISTINCT)
- GROUP BY

---

## 3. Repeat vs One-Time Customers

### Description

Classified customers based on the number of orders placed.

### Business Value

Evaluates customer retention and repeat purchasing behaviour.

### SQL Concepts Used

- Common Table Expression (CTE)
- CASE Statement
- COUNT()

---

## 4. Revenue per Customer

### Description

Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.

### Business Value

Supports customer valuation and future segmentation.

---

## 5. Revenue by Country

### Description

Summarized customer revenue by country.

### Business Value

Identifies high-performing geographic markets.

---

## 6. Customer Segmentation

### Description

Grouped customers into High Value, Medium Value and Low Value segments.

### Business Value

Supports targeted marketing campaigns and customer retention strategies.

---

# Key Findings

The analysis provides visibility into:

- Highest-value customers
- Customer purchasing behaviour
- Customer retention
- Geographic customer distribution
- Customer spending patterns

---

# Business Recommendations

- Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Improve retention campaigns for one-time buyers.
- Increase marketing investment in high-performing countries.
- Develop personalised offers for medium-value customers.
- Monitor customer purchasing trends regularly.
