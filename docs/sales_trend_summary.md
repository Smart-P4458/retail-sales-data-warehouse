# Sales Trend Analysis Summary

## Objective

Evaluate business performance over time using monthly, quarterly and cumulative sales trends.

---

## Business Questions

- How does revenue change month to month?
- Which quarter performs best?
- What is the monthly growth rate?
- What is the cumulative revenue?
- Which months perform best?

---

## Analyses Completed

- Monthly Revenue
- Monthly Quantity
- Quarterly Revenue
- Best Sales Month
- Month-over-Month Growth
- Running Total Revenue
- Highest Growth Month

---

## SQL Concepts Used

- Window Functions
- LAG()
- RANK()
- Running Totals
- Aggregation
- CTEs

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing before peak sales months.
- Investigate causes of low-performing months.
- Plan inventory using seasonal demand.
- Monitor growth trends regularly.

---

## Suggested Dashboard Visuals

- Line Chart (Monthly Revenue)
- Area Chart (Running Total)
- Column Chart (Quarterly Revenue)
- KPI Card (Growth Rate)
