# Customer Analytics

## What is Customer Analytics?

Customer Analytics is the process of using customer data to understand purchasing behaviour and support business decisions.

## Topics Covered

- Lifetime Revenue
- Order Frequency
- Customer Retention
- Revenue by Country
- Customer Segmentation

## New SQL Concept

Common Table Expressions (CTEs)

Why use CTEs?

- Improve readability
- Simplify complex queries
- Reduce repeated logic

## Key Lesson

The value of SQL is not just retrieving data—it is helping the business understand and serve its customers better.
