# Executive KPIs

## What is a KPI?

A Key Performance Indicator (KPI) is a measurable value used to evaluate business performance.

## KPIs Created

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Products
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Average Revenue per Customer
- Average Items per Order

## Key Lesson

KPIs should always answer a business question rather than simply demonstrate SQL syntax.
