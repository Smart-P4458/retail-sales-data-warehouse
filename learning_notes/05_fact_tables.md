# Fact Tables

## Definition

A Fact Table stores measurable business events.

Examples include:

- Revenue
- Quantity
- Profit
- Cost
- Sales

## Characteristics

- Stores numbers
- Contains foreign keys
- Connects dimension tables
- Used for reporting and analytics

## In This Project

Our Fact Table:

fact_sales

Measures:

- Quantity
- Revenue

Connected Dimensions:

- Customer
- Product
- Date

## Interview Tip

Question:

Why do Fact Tables use Foreign Keys?

Answer:

Foreign Keys connect business events to descriptive information stored in Dimension Tables while maintaining referential integrity.
