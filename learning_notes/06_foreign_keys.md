# Foreign Keys

A Foreign Key creates a relationship between tables.

Example

Customer

↓

Customer Key

↓

Fact Table

Benefits

- Prevents invalid records
- Enforces referential integrity
- Improves data quality
- Makes joins possible

Interview Tip

Foreign Keys ensure every business transaction references valid records in related tables.
